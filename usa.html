First US School experience

This is a photo of my friends and me at the airport saying goodbye. We took this picture two years ago when I first left Kazakhstan to study at an art boarding school in Michigan for visual arts. At that time, I was extremely excited and nervous about moving to an unfamiliar country with only my third language and no clear understanding of what would happen next. My acceptance to Milton was the next step in my journey to the United States, and I am excited for this new chapter in my life as well.
