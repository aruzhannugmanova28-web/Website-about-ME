- Visual Arts

One of my biggest passions isn’t just a hobby, it’s a lifestyle. I loooove visual art: painting, drawing, pottery/ceramics, basically making new things in both 2D and 3D. Art is something that brought me to the U.S., where I spent a year at an art boarding school in Michigan, and it makes me not only love it but believe that it's some sort of a "fate" or destiny that I should follow throughout my life. So, yes, art is what makes a big part of who I am now.

- Music

I make music too. I played piano for eight years, but I quit because it didn’t really click for me. Now I play the dombra, a national kazakh two-string instrument similar to a guitar, and both electric and acoustic guitar. Maybe one day I’ll perform at an open mic or beatnik!
